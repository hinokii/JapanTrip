Travel to Japan in Summer 2024
During our 3-week annual trip to Japan in summer 2024, we traveled to 6 locations.

Osaka is the capital of West Japan and the kitchen of Japan.
Kyoto is the old capital of Japan with lots of temples and history.
Kinosaki is famous for crabs and hot springs, located in norther part of Hyogo.
Himeji is famous for Himeji castle which is a designated national treasure.
Hakone is famous for hot springs and its nature.
Kamakura is the old capital and Shonan is famous for beaches.
